Why PCOS Awareness Matters

Polycystic Ovary Syndrome affects 1 in 10 women worldwide, yet many go undiagnosed for years. Early detection can help manage symptoms and prevent long-term health complications like diabetes and heart disease.

What is PCOS?

PCOS is a hormonal disorder causing enlarged ovaries with small cysts. It can lead to irregular periods, excess hair growth, acne, and obesity.

Common Symptoms

Look out for irregular periods, excessive hair growth, acne, weight gain, and hair thinning. Symptoms often appear around puberty.
